Perspective

Of the $6.5 trillion dollar federal budget outlined for 2024, the total budget devoted to NASA, $27.2 billion, represents only 0.5% of total spending.

In total, the JWST project has cost approximately $10 billion dollars over 26 years with the project budget starting back in 1998. However, this works out to the expendiature of roughly $384 million dollars per year during the planning, construction, and launch phases of the project. Now that the telescope has begun its observation run, yearly operating costs have fallen to $175 million.

Considering the vast benefits to not only scientific progress but to advancements in applied engineering, the United States devotes a vanishingly small portion of its resources to the field.
